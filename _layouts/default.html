About Me

Hey! My name is Sriharsha, I'm a Software Engineer with a passion for building machine learning systems with privacy in mind. My Interests mainly lie in theoretical machine learning and in improving Natural Language processing, specifically in NLU and bridging the gap between high and low resource langauges. I am also interested in improving mining algorithms to extract useful information from the web. I did my undergrad with a major in computer science from PES University.

Outside of work, I like to go for a run and play multiplayer RPG games sometime.

One Card For All

Mullen's Holiday Card 2015

In the spirit of wishing the world peace and joy during the 2015 holiday season, Mullen built a site around an algorithm that generates a holiday greeting to each and every person on the planet. We also accounted for newcomers being added at an amazing rate. Overall, the website is a tranquil, animated experience. As new names appear, visitors can watch them fall, like snowflakes, onto a stylized world map. Users can also find their own name and see it as part of the world collective.

Calendr

All-In-One Calendar/Scheduling Web App
